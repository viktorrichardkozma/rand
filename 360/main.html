ABOUT

Glowria is a magical trip happening in the darkness. A spectecular mixture of storytelling and light effects that brings the audiance into another fantasy world. With the special light art technique the light becomes paint and you suddenly find yourself in a new dimension, where the drawings, the story, the lights and the sounds merge, creating a perfect harmony. Glowria absorbs, thrills and enchants everyone. During the live performances exciting and honest stories come to life and the viewers’ imagination expands.
